ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

Aircon & Refrigeration Services. Nestled in the vibrant community of Muntinlupa, Gelo's Ice Cream stands as a beacon of sweet delight. As you step through our doors, you're welcomed into a world where every scoop is crafted with care and passion, reflecting the rich tapestry of flavors and the warmth of Filipino hospitality. At Gelo's, we take pride in sourcing the freshest local ingredients, ensuring that each bite of our artisanal ice cream is a burst of pure, unadulterated goodness. From the velvety smoothness of classic favorites like Ube and Mango to the adventurous fusion of flavors like Buko Pandan and Langka, our menu is a celebration of the diverse culinary landscape of the Philippines. But Gelo's is more than just a purveyor of fine ice cream; it's a gathering place where families, friends, and neighbors come together to share moments of joy and create lasting memories. Our cozy ambiance and friendly staff ensure that every visit is a delightful experience, whether you're enjoying a leisurely cone on a sunny afternoon or treating yourself to a decadent sundae after a long day. So come, join us at Gelo's Ice Cream in Muntinlupa, where every scoop is a celebration of flavor, community, and the simple joys of life. With our experienced technicians and commitment to customer satisfaction, we ensure that your cooling needs are met efficiently and effectively.
